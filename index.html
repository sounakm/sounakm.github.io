Sounak Mukherjee

PhD Student, Princeton University

Hello! I am Sounak, a 1st year PhD student at the Department of Electrical Engineering, Princeton University. I completed my BS-MS in Physical Sciences from Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Kolkata, India. At IISER Kolkata, I had worked on my MS project under the guidance of Prof. Nirmalya Ghosh, Department of Physical Sciences, exploring orbital angular momentum of light. My research interest lies in experimental optics with special inclination towards nanophotonics, quantum optics, and light-matter interactions in general.

Get in touch with me at: sounakm [at] princeton [dot] edu
